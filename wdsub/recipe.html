Cookie Recipe

  1. Prep Work

    • Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
    • WASH YOUR HANDS
    • Line baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside

  2. Ingredients

    • 1 cup of softened butter
    • 1 cup granulated suger
    • 1 cup of light borwn suger,packed
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 2 large eggs
    • 3 cups all purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1 teaspoon sea salt
    • 2 cups choca chips

  3. Instructions

    1. In a medium bowl mix:
      • flour
      • baking soda
      • baking powder
      • salt
    2. cream together: butter and sugers until combined
    3. beat in eggs and vanilla until light
    4. mix flour mixture until combined (DONT OVERMIX)
    5. mix in chocolate chips
    6. roll into balls (2-3tbsp)
    7. Bake for 8-10 minutes
    8. let cool for 5 minutes
    9. Finally, ENJOY!

  4. Extra Info

    • Bakes 36 cookies (2tblp of dough
    • Total time: 30min
